#  Commercial Performance Dashboard – 2023 vs 2024
📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Commercial & Sales Performance Analysis dashboard built in Power BI, comparing performance between 2023 and 2024.

The goal was to simulate a real telecom/commercial business environment and analyze:

Revenue vs Target performance

Customer Growth & Retention

Churn behavior

Sales productivity & regional performance

Customer segmentation impact on revenue

🎯 Business Objectives

Analyze revenue performance against targets

Understand customer growth & churn patterns

Measure sales reps productivity and regional efficiency

Identify high-risk customer segments

Evaluate year-over-year performance change (2023 vs 2024)

📈 Key Insights
🔹 Revenue Performance

2024 Revenue: 2.38M

Target: 2.4M

Variance: -23K (-1%)

Contribution Margin: 60%

Highest Revenue Region: Delta (46%)

🔹 Customer Growth & Retention

Active Customers: 1,852

Retention Rate: 85.6%

Churn: 14.4%

Highest Active Region: Delta

Churn increased in 2024 vs 2023 (52% vs 48%)

🔹 Sales Rep Performance

MTD Achievement: 97.5%

Best Performing Region: Giza (103.6%)

Top Performing Rep exceeded target by more than 24%

Direct Sales Channel contributes 52% of total revenue
